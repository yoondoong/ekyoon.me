DOC Carbon Emissions

Visualizing real-time carbon emissions data of the Dartmouth Outing Club through fun visuals. Design aims to be clean, engaging, credible, motivating and inspiring. Key features include: data visualization with parallax illustrations, carbon emissions calculator, an about page and most importantly, an easy backend data maintenence.

Online Mafia Game

Online platform to play your all-time-favorite Mafia game with your Facebook friends! Design aims to be mafia-like, friendly, easy and coherent. Key features include: Facebook authentification, live chat, randomized role distribution and a synchronized game!

Protein Structure Search

Website users can easily search similar protein structures through a certain algorithm even with no coding background. Design aims to be professional, coherent and intuitive. Key features include: the search and downloading the results

Trend Surfer (Data Marketing Korea Intern)

Prototype of the "Trend Surfer" created during internship at Data Marketing Korea. Users can see an ordered list of keywords by how much they are "trending" on the internet as well as other related keywords. Design aims to have clearly prioritized features, Key features include: exploring the keyword trend, more detailed analysis of certain keywords and a dashboard.

CDJ Illustration (Data Marketing Korea Intern)

Illustration of the Consumer Decision Journey(CDJ). Dots represent potential customers and they are filtered as they move from search engines, SNS sites, and e-commerce sites. Illustration was used during presentations with clients for Data Marketing Korea. Created using Sketch.

Snooze Trio

Snooze Trio was designed after my two best friends and myself. Freshman year was a very hard time for me that I missed my friends from high school very much. It inspired me to draw scenes where the trio would hang out together once more with no woes or worries whatsoever. Drawing and desgining them was like a little snooze for me, where I could forget about reality for a short period of time. As much as I feel attached to the Trio, I hope to, one day, introduce them properly to the public.
